Strengthen Brand/Style Guide

Color

Strengthen uses strategic colors to give users a fluid, easy to view experience.

Colors should provide good contrast, clarity, and ease of viewing. Colors can be used to give users a heirarchy of importance in actions. Use colors to direct users to action, show importance (red alert text), and provide easy communication.

Typography

Strengthen uses typography to help show heirarchy of information.

Our typography will champion legibility & readability, provide a clear focus for users, and be aesthetically pleasing across all devices.
